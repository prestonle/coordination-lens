CoordinationLens - Experimental AI Divergence Detector

⚠️ RESEARCH PROTOTYPE - NOT FOR PRODUCTION USE ⚠️

What it does:
• Manual comparison of AI responses (copy/paste required)
• Basic word-overlap detection using Jaccard similarity
• Visual indicator when responses differ significantly

What it does NOT do:
• NO automated capture from AI websites
• NO validated accuracy
• NO real-time monitoring

For researchers and developers only.
All processing local. No data collection.
